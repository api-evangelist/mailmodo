This review examines Constant Contact, an email marketing platform that has seen declining market share since 2020. While the tool offers user-friendly design, strong deliverability rates, and extensive integrations, it suffers from limited automation capabilities, high pricing relative to features, and difficult account cancellation processes. The article concludes that alternatives like Mailmodo may provide better value for users with large contact lists or advanced automation needs.
